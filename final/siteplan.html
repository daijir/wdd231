Site Plan for Final Project

Site Name: Chinese Restaurant - Hamafuji

Reason: "Hamafuji" is the actual restaurant name that my friend (brother Hamada) run.

Example Domain: hamafuji.jp (Assuming Japan-focused business)

Site Purpose

The official website for "Chinese Restaurant - Hamafuji" aims to attract customers and enhance the customer experience by conveying the authentic and warm appeal of our Chinese cuisine to the local community. Specifically, we will provide the latest menu information, and offer an online reservation feature. Our goal is to create an information hub that provides peace of mind and anticipation, making customers want to say, "I want to come back again!"

  • Communicate Culinary Appeal: Visually appeal to customers with beautiful photos and detailed descriptions of our delicious dishes and unique qualities.
  • Provide Information: Offer comprehensive information necessary for a visit, such as opening hours, regular holidays, access details, and private party availability.
  • Online Reservations: Provide a convenient system for customers to easily book tables, enhancing accessibility.
  • Customer Engagement: Build trust and establish a friendly restaurant

Scenarios

Anticipate questions a site visitor might have and consider how they connect to the website's content.

Scenario 1: A father looking for a family dinner spot

  • "I want to eat out with my family this weekend, but are there Chinese dishes my kids will eat?"
  • "Can I go without a reservation, or can I check table availability online?"
  • "Is there parking available? Can I bring a stroller inside?"

Scenario 2: A business person looking for a workplace lunch

  • "What's the menu of this restaurant like?"
  • "Is it a place where I can easily eat alone? Do they have counter seating?"
  • "Can I get takeout? I'd like to check the menu beforehand."

Color Schema

aim for a color schema that expresses "warmth" and "authenticity"

  • Primary color: Orange
  • Secondary color: Yellow
  • Background color: Gray
  • Text color: Dark Gray

Typography

aim for a typography that suits to English letter and Chinese Restaurant style

  • h1, h2, h3 font-family: 'Playfair Display'
  • body font-family: 'Open Sans'
